Raw Interview recording of Cheuk Kwan and Lily Cho in conversation on Cheuk Kwan's series Eating Chinese (2006)

Raw interview footage between Lily Cho and Cheuk Kwan on Kwan's 15 part documentary series called Chinese Restaurants. This interview was conducted for a chapter in the first Reel Asian Anthology, Reel Asian: Asian Canada on Screen.
All of Chinese Restaurants can be found on Cheuk's youtube channel: https://www.youtube.com/cheukckwan
